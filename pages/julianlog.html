Julian's Log
What was my role?
My role was to manage the group, and teach them about both Git and GitHub.
The group was hard to manage, but I did eventually finish the website as I worked at home.
Because of this, I became the main contributor for this project.
What did I do?
I did everything from the website structure, to the nav-bar and even the 3D graphics.
Although, not all was my doing, like for instance the separate logs.
I must also admit that I did replace much of what my group actually did,
and I therefore recommend that you take a look at the commit history to see what everyone did.
What problems did I face?
The main problem I faced was definitely the lack of productivity in the group,
and I was always busy just helping people, instead of working myself.
I also had some problems with how the javascript wouldn't load,
and I eventually fixed this issue by using a tool called Rollup.
